# Intelligence Routing

Use this folder for decisions, research, market notes, and archived observations.

- `decisions/` stores durable decisions and why they were made.
- `research/` stores external or internal research notes.
- `archive/` stores inactive intelligence.

Promote current operating facts into `Context/` or project status files.
